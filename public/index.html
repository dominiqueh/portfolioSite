WHO AM I?

A person of dual identities. Born and raised in New York city, with childhood memories of bypassing skyscrapers on walks to Central Park with my mother and getting knishes from Pete the Hot Dog Man for lunch. These memories stand beside those of England's countryside where I'd attempt to clothe the free-roaming cows of the New Forest with a Yankees hat while on holiday visiting relatives.

A technological unicorn, my educational background is a dual-major of Psychology and French language as well as well as certificates in web development. While studying psychology and French, I was driven by a fascination in learning the systems by which we make decisions and communicate. After taking a certificate course at NYU, I found that programming incorporated all the elements that I loved from both fields while providing the cognitive challenge I relish and want out of a job.
